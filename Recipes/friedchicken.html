Crispy Fried Chicken

Now the websites writer has had all sorts of experiences with this recipe: undercooked, overcooked, slighty bloody. It seems as though no matter how many times the writer attempts to make this dish, they fail miserbly and resort to ordering take out.

But you, dear reader, might have better a shot at making something edible. Provided, you follow the recipe bellow!

    Ingredients:
  • Like 3 drumsticks
  • 1 and a half cups of flour
  • Chicken seasonings mix
  • Eggs
  • Quarter cup cornstarch

Follow the below steps, and pray you end up with something edible

  1. Prepare the wet batter by whipping 3 eggs in a bowl, along with some of the seasonings mix
  2. Prepare the dry batter by mixing the flour, cornstarch and seasonings mix
  3. Dunk the drumsticks in the wet batter, then coat them in the dry batter
  4. Fry the drumsticks in light-medium hot vegetable oil for around 17 minutes

Voila!
