Garlic Butter Pasta

Looking for a quick dinner meal? that was what our writer was looking for when he attempted this dish. While he did manage to create an edible, and actually tasty dish, he made the mistake of adding too much spice to it, leaving them with regret the next day.

This dish is sort of an "eye it out" dish. The ingredients can never be truly measured, and you can always toss in whatever leftovers you have.

    Ingredients:
  • Pasta of any shape or size
  • Garlic
  • Unsweetend Butter
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Paprika

Follow the steps below, and have a fire extingushier at ready.

  1. Boil your in salty water pasta until cooked
  2. In a pan, add your butter and let it melt
  3. Once all the butter is melted, add your chopped garlic
  4. Add your cooked pasta, along with half a cup of the pasta water
  5. Add your pepper and paprika to the mix and enjoy!
